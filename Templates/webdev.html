Olalekan Rahman's Profile

Historical Background

Olalekan Rahman Abiodun was born at Olomodudu's compound Ekunle, Iseyin in Oyo state Nigeria on the 10th of June 1981 to the family of Mr AbdulHammed Olalekan Adeleke and Mrs Medinat Ayanlola-Olalekan

Academic History

Mr Olalekan attended Methodist Primary School Ago-Ijio Iseyin and then proceeded to Iseyin District Grammar School Iseyin. Having failed to secure a credit in English Language in his West African Senior School Examinations, he re-enrolled at Ansar-Deen Grammar School Opapa Oyo where he made a C4 in English Language. He then proceeded to the University of Lagos Nigeria for his BSc in Petroleum and Gas Engineering and MSc(2019) in Biomedical Engineering. He had Distinction in his MSc and conducted research in Optimization of Diagnosis and Management of Schizophrenia using Data Mining Algorithms.
